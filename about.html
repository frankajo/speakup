About Us

SpeakUp is a collection of professional service provisers and official that respond as a group and in a timely fashion to various needs of domestic violence and sexual violence survivors by providing legal, medical, councelling and psychological support. It is to encourage any individual who is passing through any form of domestic or sexual violence to SpeakUp.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a society in which domestic violence no longer exists.

SpeakUp seek to prevent domestic and sexual violence by advocating for positive changes in the societal systems that may lead to oppression,
fostering a better understanding of domestic and sexual violence through our different programs,
and providing culturally relevant direct services that
empower victims of abuse.




Vision

SpeakUp vision is that every community in which we serve can take pride in having
safe homes and safe relationships. We are committed to provide help and hope to all who have been deprived of these basic
human rights and we are dedicated to work collaboratively to build strong, positive,
nonviolent communities.

Many children exposed to violence in the home are also victims of physical abuse.1 Children who witness domestic violence or are victims of abuse themselves are at serious risk for long-term physical and mental health problems.2 Children who witness violence between parents may also be at greater risk of being violent in their future relationships. If you are a parent who is experiencing abuse, it can be difficult to know how to protect your child.
